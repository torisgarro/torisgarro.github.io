Customization & Personalization

We’re exploring customization and personalization features to encourage readers to form habits with The Atlantic’s journalism.

Year: 2021-2022
My role: Senior product designer
In development

The Problem

Customization (when a user chooses their experience to meet their need) and personalization (when a product delivers an experience to a user in anticipation of their need) can create more relevant experiences for readers, encouraging habitual use and improving retention. However, many readers love The Atlantic for its editorial judgement – trusting editors to curate the most important stories. How might we serve readers who want a more relevant experience via customization and/or personalization, without alienating readers who value editorial curation (our biggest fans)?
