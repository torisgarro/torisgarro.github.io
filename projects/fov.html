Dates

Jan 2019 - Jun 2019

Design Team

Fei Liu (product designer at The Intercept)

Field of Vision is a filmmaker-driven documentary unit, and a project of First Look Media. We designed an online security guide for documentary filmmakers.

The Problem

Documentary filmmakers often contact Field of Vision and Freedom of The Press Foundation for information about and training on digital security. In order to answer some of the most often asked questions in a more accessible way, Field of Vision and Freedom of The Press Foundation wanted to create an online guide to the most up-to-date information on security resources.

Discovery

The editorial team kicked off the project with a brief outline of the content they hoped to fill out for the guide. I translated this Word doc’s structure into a diagram to help visualize the content’s information architecture. We also interviewed stakeholders from Freedom of the Press Foundation, who had already inteviewed filmmakers about their digital security practices and needs for the site’s content.
