At What Cost?

I created this site for my final project in General Assembly’s front-end web development course. The site is a tool for designers to consider the ethical implications of their work.

Background

Ethics in design is a profound and messy topic. I wanted to present this issue in a way that is digestible and actionable for users, by focusing on real-world examples and replicable exercises. The intended user is a product designer concerned about ethics, and the main call to action is to engage this users’ team and other stakeholders in the exercises I created.
