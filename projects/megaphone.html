Megaphone

Megaphone is a dynamic ad platform for podcasts. I led the design of new features that allow publishers to reconcile projected and delivered revenue in the platform.

Background

At Panoply, our own producers and ad operations team use Megaphone to produce and monetize content. Both the Panoply team and our enterprise clients requested that we add billing functionality into our platform, in order to eliminate the need for third-party software like Google’s Double Click for Publishers. This situation challenged us to create features for both in-house and client use, while not catering too specifically to one team’s needs over another’s.

Research

I interviewed Panoply’s ad operations and financial teams to understand their current workflow for revenue reconciliation. I also interviewed our account managers who are more familiar with the workflow of our enterprise clients. Then I created user flows for how I imagined different user types would interact with new billing features in Megaphone. This allowed me to define the new screens I would need to design for this project and to identify the existing screens I would need to improve.
