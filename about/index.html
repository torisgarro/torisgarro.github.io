About

Hi, I'm Tori! As a product designer, I use my strengths in empathy, openness and connection to design experiences with intention and impact. I am passionate about designing digital products that improve our offline lives.

I currently design for First Look Media. Previously, I worked at Panoply Media (Slate), Morning Consult and National Geographic.

Before pursuing a career in design, I studied comparative literature, foreign languages and journalism. I learned the importance of empathizing with others’ perspectives across backgrounds and cultures, questioning my own assumptions, and considering the larger context of my work. These skills make up the foundation of my approach to design today.
