About

I’m a product designer with a background in research and writing. After studying comparative literature at Washington University in St. Louis, I moved to Italy to pursue my interests in photography and design. Since then, I’ve worked as a designer for startups in New York and DC, including First Look Media, Panoply Media and Morning Consult.

I’m most interested in how design and tech impact behavior, and what that means for our future. I believe in designing products that align with users’ values and well-being, and that improve the systems and societies in which we live.
