About

I’m a product designer with a background in research and writing. After studying comparative literature at Washington University in St. Louis, I moved to Italy to pursue my interests in photography and design. Since then, I’ve worked as a designer in New York and DC.

I’m most interested in understanding the political and economic context of design, so I'm currently studying for a master’s in economics at The New School for Social Research.
